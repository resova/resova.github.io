Explore Kashmir

DAY 1

Arrive at Srinagar. Our representative will meet, assist and transfer you to Nigeen Residency Resort, Srinagar. Welcome drink upon arrival, followed by free individual activities for an hour. Day reserved for local sightseeing - visit the majestic Dal Lake and enjoy a shikara ride. Cover six important points in Dal Lake. Overnight stay at Resort, Srinagar.

DAY 2

DAY 3

Visit the famous Dachigam National Park in the morning after breakfast. The sanctuary, which is 22 km from Srinagar, covers an area of 141 sq km. The park has been a protected area since 1910 and is famous for its coniferous forests and for 'hangul' or Kashmiri stag. Back to Srinagar in the evening and overnight stay at Resort.

DAY 4

Drive to Manasbal Lake in the morning, said to be the deepest lake in the Kashmir Valley at a depth of 13 m/43 feet. Manasbal is famous for its aquatic birds and the lotuses that bloom here in the months of July and August. You can enjoy a shikara ride and take in the serene beauty of the clear, blue water and surrounding mountains. Back to Srinagar in the evening and overnight stay at Resort.

DAY 7

Proceed to Hazratbal Shrine, situated on the left bank of Dal Lake. The shrine contains a relic believed to be a hair of the Islamic prophet Mohammed. Also visit Naseem Bagh, which is famous for its chinar trees, by Dal Lake and Hari Parbat, a hill with a fort, overlooking Srinagar. Visit the leading shopping malls in Srinagar and shop to your heart's content. Overnight stay at Resort.

DAY 8

Yusmarg in Kashmiri means The Meadow of Jesus. It is believed by the natives that Jesus came to Kashmir and stayed at Yusmarg for some time. It is an alpine valley covered with snow clad mountains and the meadows of Pine and Fir, It lies 13 km south of Charari Sharief a town of Budgam district of Jammu and Kashmir. It is situated at the bank of Doodganga River which is a tributory of Jehlum River. It is a popular tourist destination, nestled within the imposing Pir Panjal peaks, a sub range of Himalaya. The Peaks are: the Sunset Peak and the Tatakooti Peak for which the expeditions lead from this hill station. It lies at an altitude of 2396 metres above sea level.

Begin your day with a visit to the Shankaracharya temple, an ancient Shiva temple located on Gopadari Hill. Visit the delightful Mughal gardens in Srinagar and its vicinity including Chashma Shahi (the royal fountains), Pari Mahal (the palace of fairies), Nishat Bagh, Shalimar Bagh, Harwan garden and Tulip Garden. Go shopping for Kashmiri handicrafts in the evening.

DAY 10

Breakfast and transfer to airport. Go back home with memories of a lifetime.
